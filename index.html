About Us

Welcome to LJ Mothers Lap — Special School for Unique Minds

LJ Mothers Lap is a dedicated special education center in Salem committed to empowering children with developmental challenges like Autism, ADHD, Intellectual Disability and Learning Disability.

Our mission is to help every child reach their full potential through personalized care, therapy support, and inclusive learning.

"Every child is special. Every child can learn."

Who We Support

  • Autism Spectrum (ASD)
  • ADHD
  • Intellectual Disability
  • Speech Delay
  • Learning Disabilities
  • Behavioral Challenges

Through personalized education plans, certified therapists, and a loving environment, we spread kindness and support to every child and family.

Our Care
& Support

We provide compassionate care and personalized support to help every child grow with confidence.

Special Education

Personalized learning plans tailored to each child's unique needs, fostering academic growth at their own pace.

Our special educators work one-on-one and in small groups using multi-sensory teaching methods. IEP goals are tracked regularly and shared with families to ensure consistent progress at home and school.

Event Celebrations

Birthdays, festivals and special occasions celebrated together — creating joyful memories for every child.

We celebrate every child's birthday, along with festivals like Pongal, Diwali, Christmas and Children's Day. These celebrations build a sense of belonging, joy and community — making every child feel truly special and valued.
Read More →

Gym & Fitness

Fun physical activities and exercises designed to build strength, coordination and healthy habits in children.

Our gym sessions are specially adapted for children of all abilities. Activities include balance training, obstacle courses and group sports that improve gross motor skills while keeping children active and happy.
Read More →

Visual Learning

Interactive visual aids and tools that help children absorb and retain concepts through sight-based techniques.

We use picture cards, visual schedules, color-coded materials and digital boards to support children who learn best through images. This approach is especially effective for non-verbal and early communication learners.
Read More →

Dance

Creative dance sessions that boost confidence, rhythm and self-expression in a joyful group environment.

Dance therapy helps children develop body awareness, improve coordination and express emotions non-verbally. Sessions include classical, folk and freestyle dance adapted to each child's comfort level and physical ability.
Read More →

Silambam

Traditional Tamil martial art training that builds discipline, focus and physical agility in young learners.

Silambam is a centuries-old Tamil martial art using bamboo sticks. Our trained instructors teach children in a safe, structured way that builds concentration, self-discipline, hand-eye coordination and cultural pride.
Read More →

Drawing

Art sessions that nurture creativity and fine motor skills, allowing children to express themselves freely.

Drawing and art therapy give children a powerful outlet for emotions and ideas. Sessions cover sketching, colouring, craft and painting — all designed to strengthen fine motor control and boost creative thinking.
Read More →

Monthly Trip

Exciting educational outings every month to parks, museums and nature spots — learning beyond the classroom.

Every month we organise a supervised outing to places like nature parks, zoos, science museums and cultural landmarks. These trips build social skills, curiosity and real-world learning experiences children remember forever.
Read More →

School Syllabus

Curriculum-aligned academic support helping children keep pace with school subjects through guided learning.

We follow the Tamil Nadu state board and CBSE syllabus to support children in Maths, Science, English and Tamil. Our educators simplify concepts using adaptive methods so every child can understand and keep up with their class.
