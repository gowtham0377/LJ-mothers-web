About Us

Welcome to Lj Mothers Lap - Special School for unique Minds

Lj Mothers Lap is a dedicated special education center in salem committed to empoweroing children with developmental challenges like Autism, ADHD, intellectual Disability and learning Disability.

Our mission is to help every child reach their full potential through personalized care, therapy support, and inclusive learning.
